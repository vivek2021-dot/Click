Transforming Traditional Networks into Agile, Secure, and High-Performance Infrastructures that Drive Cloud Adoption, Enhance User Experience, and Reduce Operational Complexity

Transforming Your Network

With the rise of Cloud computing and "as-a-service" technologies, networks need a significant upgrade to deliver smooth, reliable application performance. Traditional WAN architectures often face limitations—struggling with increased traffic, complex management, and potential security vulnerabilities. SD-WAN addresses these challenges by intelligently routing traffic, optimizing bandwidth usage, and ensuring secure, high-performance connectivity. It reduces operating costs, simplifies management, and enhances user experience, enabling businesses to fully leverage modern digital solutions without compromising on reliability or security.

Traditional vs. Smart Networking

Traditional WAN Limitations

Traditional WANs use expensive connections such as leased lines and Multiprotocol Label Switching (MPLS), which are incompatible with today’s cloud-centric and mobile-first enterprises. They require significant infrastructure and manual programming to connect complex networks—making them expensive, time-consuming, and prone to errors.

SD-WAN: Smarter, Faster, Lower Costs

Software-defined WAN uses software-defined networking concepts to let IT work smarter, faster, and at lower costs! SD-WAN greatly simplifies the process by automatically determining the most effective way to route traffic across different offices and data center sites using business-defined rules from a central management portal. It brings Agility, Flexibility, and Security—all at a lower cost.

Secure Cloud Connectivity

Using expensive MPLS services to connect globally is a huge cost for companies with multiple locations. Most organizations using traditional WAN continuously upgrade their bandwidth to ensure reliable connectivity.

SD-WAN reduces networking costs by leveraging inexpensive internet connections and dynamically selecting from a cost-efficient mix of public internet and private connections.

It also intelligently routes traffic directly to the cloud without unnecessary backhaul, improving speed and reducing latency. Built-in firewall capabilities ensure robust security for cloud-based applications.

Benefits of Using SD-WAN

Enhanced Availability, Capacity, and Resilience

Automates traffic routing and intelligently switches to backup connections during failures to ensure uninterrupted productivity.

Disparate Connectivity at Reduced Cost

Reduces dependency on costly MPLS links by using a combination of public and private connections.

Secure Cloud Access

Directs traffic to cloud services without detours, improving speed and reliability.

Centralized Management

Configure and monitor the entire network from a single portal.
