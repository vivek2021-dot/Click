Keeping Your Business Moving, Maintenance After Maintenance.

Our Commitment to Your IT Health

Keeping your IT systems healthy and secure is essential for uninterrupted business operations. Our Annual Maintenance Contracts offer a complete package of proactive care, rapid support, and performance optimization for your IT infrastructure. With regular maintenance, monitoring, and expert assistance, we ensure your systems stay reliable, secure, and ready to perform — all year round.

Key Inclusions

Scheduled Preventive Maintenance

Regular inspections, cleaning, hardware diagnostics, and system optimization to prevent failures and downtime.

Priority Technical Support

Faster response times for AMC clients, with both remote troubleshooting and on-site assistance when needed.

Software & Security Updates

Timely installation of OS updates, security patches, firewall rules, and antivirus definitions to safeguard systems.

Data Backup & Recovery

Automated backups, cloud storage integration, and tested recovery plans to ensure business continuity in case of data loss.

Hardware & Network Health Checks

Monitoring and diagnostics for servers, PCs, printers, storage devices, switches, routers, and wireless networks.

Performance & Security Audits

Regular audits to identify performance bottlenecks, security vulnerabilities, and compliance gaps.

Remote Monitoring & Alerts

24x7 monitoring tools to detect and fix issues proactively before they impact operations.

Asset & License Management

Tracking of IT hardware, warranties, and software licenses with timely renewal reminders.

Genuine Parts Replacement

Use of manufacturer-approved components for repairs and replacements to maintain system reliability.

Reporting & Documentation

Monthly or quarterly reports summarizing maintenance activities, incidents, and improvement recommendations.

Clarity & Transparency

Proactive & Reactive Support

Scalable & Flexible Plans
