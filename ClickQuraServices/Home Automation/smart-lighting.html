Smart Lighting & Voice Control

Intelligent lighting. Simple control. Smarter workspace.


In today's fast-paced business world, every little bit counts—and that goes for your lighting, too. Our Smart Lighting & Voice Control solutions are more than on/off switches, though. They help create a smart, efficient, and dynamic office that boosts productivity, reduces expenses, and enhances overall employee health. Through combining high-tech technology with user-friendly control, we provide a hassle-free lighting solution designed specifically for your business needs.

Why Smart Lighting is a Smart Business Decision

Energy Efficiency & Cost Savings

Our solution utilises high-efficiency LED technology and intelligent automation. Motion sensors, daylight harvesting, and timed lighting all ensure the lights are only lit when and where needed. This foresight-driven strategy minimizes energy usage and results in considerable savings on your electricity bill.

Increased Productivity & Employee Well-being

The correct light can be a game-changer. We can install "Human-Centric Lighting" systems that emulate natural daylight cycles, changing color temperature and brightness over the course of the day. This has been shown to control circadian rhythms, cut eye strain, and enhance employee mood, concentration, and overall health.

Ultimate Convenience & Flexibility

In your hands. Control your entire lighting system from one app on your smartphone or tablet. For even more convenience, add voice control to enable employees to make lighting settings changes with the sound of their voice. Hands-free operation is ideal for big offices, conference rooms, and collaborative workspaces.

Optimized Space & Security

Design personalized "scenes" for various activities—a light setting for a team meeting, dimmed for a presentation, or a lively glow for an event. Smart lighting also enhances security through the deployment of motion sensors and scheduled timing to repel intruders and keep the facility brightly lit in all areas of importance.

Data-Driven Insights

Our sophisticated systems can offer rich data on energy usage and occupancy patterns. Upon analyzing these data, you are in a position to make more informed decisions regarding space use and identify new ways to further improve energy consumption.
